### (ii) value_count()

- It can be applied in both Series and DataFrame.
- Return a Series containing the frequency of each distinct row in the DataFrame.
- **Syntax** - `DataFrame.value_count()`

``` py
marks = pd.DataFrame([
    [100,80,10],
    [90,70,7],
    [120,100,14],
    [80,70,14],
    [80,70,14]
],columns=['iq','marks','package'])

marks

marks.value_counts()

# Output
iq   marks  package
80   70     14         2
100  80     10         1
90   70     7          1
120  100    14         1
Name: count, dtype: int64
```
